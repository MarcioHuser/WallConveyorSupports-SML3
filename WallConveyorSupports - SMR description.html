Just close and reopen the shop and they will be there Answering requests to continue/remake the now discontinued Wall Conveyors mod, this mod adds six new wall conveyor support variations: - One with three conveyor connections side by side - One with two conveyor connections, with gap between the two connections - One with two conveyor connections, far from the wall - One with two conveyor connections, close to the wall - One with a single conveyor connection, 6 m away from the wall - One with a single conveyor connection, 4 m away from the wall - The milestone for will be available at the AWESOME Shop, for 6 coupons
